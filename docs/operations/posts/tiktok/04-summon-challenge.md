# TikTok #04 — Summon Challenge（互动挑战）

> 时长：15-20 秒
> 风格：快节奏模板 + 邀请参与
> 目标：发起 #SummonChallenge，制造 UGC 内容

---

## 脚本

### 画面 + 旁白

```
[0-3s] 钩子
画面：黑屏，大字打出
字幕：If you could SUMMON anything into an AI agent, what would it be?
音效：魔法音效

[3-5s] 示例 1
画面：一只猫 → 闪光 → 聊天气泡 "Stop working. Pet me. NOW."
字幕：🐱 Your cat
音效：猫叫 + 变身音效

[5-7s] 示例 2
画面：一辆旧车 → 闪光 → 聊天气泡 "My oil hasn't been changed in 8 months. I'm dying."
字幕：🚗 Your car
音效：引擎声 + 变身音效

[7-9s] 示例 3
画面：一个年度目标便签 → 闪光 → 聊天气泡 "You said you'd lose 10kg. It's March. You gained 2."
字幕：🎯 Your goals
音效：闹钟声 + 变身音效

[9-12s] 示例 4
画面：冰箱 → 闪光 → 聊天气泡 "There's expired milk in here. Again."
字幕：🧊 Your fridge
音效：冰箱嗡嗡声 + 变身音效

[12-16s] 你的选择
画面：「SUMMON」按钮 + 问号
字幕：Your turn. What would YOU summon? 🐙
旁白："Comment what you'd summon. Best ones get featured."
音效：期待音效

[16-18s] CTA
画面：#SummonChallenge 标签 + OpenOctopus logo
字幕：#SummonChallenge 🐙 Follow for more
```

### 配乐建议

- 快节奏电子/魔法风 BGM
- 每次「闪光变身」配同一个标志性音效（建立听觉记忆）

---

## 视频制作提示词

### 实体 → Agent 变身通用模板

#### 猫变身

```
A photo of a tabby cat sitting on a desk, looking annoyed. A flash of cyan (#00D4AA) light sweeps across the image from left to right. After the flash, the cat photo now has a holographic UI overlay — a chat bubble appears next to the cat saying "Stop working. Pet me. NOW." with a purple (#6C3FA0) border. The cat's eyes glow slightly cyan. Vertical 9:16. 2 seconds. Dark background.
```

#### 车变身

```
A photo of an older sedan car parked in a driveway. A flash of cyan (#00D4AA) light sweeps across. After the flash, the car has a holographic dashboard overlay showing stats — oil level (red, critical), tire pressure, mileage. A chat bubble says "My oil hasn't been changed in 8 months." with distressed emoji. Vertical 9:16. 2 seconds. Dark background.
```

#### 目标便签变身

```
A sticky note on a wall that reads "2026 Goals: Lose 10kg, Read 24 books, Save $5000". A flash of cyan light. After the flash, the note transforms into a holographic agent card with a progress bar (showing 5% progress), a disappointed face avatar, and a chat bubble: "It's March. You gained 2kg." Vertical 9:16. 2 seconds. Dark background.
```

#### 冰箱变身

```
A modern refrigerator in a kitchen. A flash of cyan (#00D4AA) light. After the flash, the fridge door has a holographic display showing its contents — items with expiration dates, some items highlighted in red (expired). A chat bubble from the fridge avatar: "There's expired milk in here. Again." Vertical 9:16. 2 seconds.
```
