# TikTok #02 — Realm Explainer（概念科普）

> 时长：30-40 秒
> 风格：快节奏信息图动画 + 旁白
> 目标：传递 Realm 分域概念

---

## 脚本

### 画面 + 旁白

```
[0-3s] 钩子
画面：一个人对着手机说话，屏幕上是一个巨大的聊天窗口，消息混乱（法律、宠物、财务、工作全混在一起）
旁白："Your AI assistant right now: one chatbox for your ENTIRE life."
字幕：One chatbox. Your entire life. 😵

[3-6s] 问题放大
画面：聊天窗口上的消息越来越多、越来越乱，最后"爆炸"成碎片
旁白："Legal questions next to pet food. Tax advice next to gym plans."
字幕：Legal 🔀 Pet food 🔀 Tax 🔀 Gym

[6-10s] 转折
画面：碎片在黑暗中重组，一只章鱼从深处升起，触手展开
旁白："What if your AI worked like an octopus?"
字幕：What if AI worked like 🐙 ?
音效：低音 transition + 水下音效

[10-18s] 章鱼隐喻
画面：章鱼的 8 条触手逐一亮起，每条连接一个域卡片
旁白："Each tentacle is a Realm — an independent domain of your life. Pet realm. Finance realm. Work realm. Legal realm. Each with its own AI team and memory."
字幕：（随旁白逐个弹出域名称）
  🐾 Pet → 💰 Finance → 💼 Work → ⚖️ Legal
  🏠 Home → 🏥 Health → 👨‍👩‍👦 Family → ❤️ Partner

[18-24s] 演示
画面：用户说 "I'm going on vacation"，信息同时路由到 4 个域
旁白："Say one thing. Every realm responds."
字幕：One input → 4 realms respond simultaneously

[24-30s] 对比
画面：左右分屏 — 左边是混乱的单一聊天框（红色调），右边是整齐的多域面板（蓝紫调）
旁白："Stop chatting. Start governing."
字幕：Chatbox ❌ → Realm Matrix ✅

[30-37s] CTA
画面：OpenOctopus logo 浮现 + 域面板全览
旁白："OpenOctopus. Organize life by realms. Open source."
字幕：🐙 OpenOctopus | Link in bio
```

---

## 视频制作提示词

### 章鱼升起 + 触手展开片段（6-10s）

```
A deep ocean scene, dark blue-black background. A stylized cartoon octopus rises slowly from the bottom of the frame, its body glowing in deep purple (#6C3FA0). As it reaches the center, its 8 tentacles spread outward gracefully, each tentacle tip lighting up in cyan (#00D4AA). Bioluminescent particles float around it. The movement is majestic and smooth. Vertical 9:16 ratio. Dark, cinematic underwater atmosphere. 4 seconds duration.
```

### 触手连接域卡片片段（10-18s）

```
Top-down view of a stylized octopus with 8 extended tentacles on a dark background (#0D1117). One by one, each tentacle tip touches a floating holographic card that lights up: first a paw icon card labeled "Pet", then a dollar icon "Finance", then a briefcase "Work", then a gavel "Legal", continuing until all 8 are connected. Each card glows in cyan (#00D4AA) with a dark blue (#1E3A5F) background when activated. Smooth sequential animation. Vertical 9:16. 6 seconds.
```

### 多域同时响应动画（18-24s）

```
A dark phone screen interface. At the top, a user message bubble says "I'm going on vacation". Below, the screen splits into 4 quadrants with a smooth animation. Each quadrant represents a realm — Pet (paw icon, cyan border), Family (heart, pink border), Vehicle (car, blue border), Finance (chart, green border). Each quadrant simultaneously shows a response bubble sliding in. Energy lines connect all quadrants back to the central message. Vertical 9:16. 4 seconds.
```

### 对比分屏（24-30s）

```
A vertical split screen (9:16). Left half: a chaotic single chat window with dozens of overlapping message bubbles in different colors, text too small to read, looking overwhelming. A red "X" stamp appears over it. Right half: a clean dark dashboard with 4x3 grid of organized realm cards, each with a clear icon and green status indicator. A green checkmark appears over it. Label left: "Chatbox", label right: "Realm Matrix". Clean transition from chaos to order. 3 seconds.
```
