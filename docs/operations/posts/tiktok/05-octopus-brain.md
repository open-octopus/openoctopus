# TikTok #05 — Octopus Brain（科普向）

> 时长：40-60 秒
> 风格：科普叙事 + 产品映射
> 目标：生物学钩子 + 品牌故事植入

---

## 脚本

### 画面 + 旁白

```
[0-4s] 钩子
画面：真实章鱼水下镜头（可用免费素材 Pexels/Pixabay）
旁白："An octopus has 500 million neurons."
字幕：500,000,000 neurons 🧠

[4-8s] 生物事实
画面：章鱼触手特写，触手在独立探索
旁白："350 million of them are in the tentacles. Not the brain."
字幕：70% of neurons in the tentacles, not the brain

[8-12s] 独立性
画面：一条被切断的触手还在移动（动画/示意图）
旁白："Cut off a tentacle — it keeps moving for an hour. Each arm thinks for itself."
字幕：Each tentacle has its own "mini-brain" 🧠

[12-16s] 协作
画面：章鱼全身优雅地游动，8 条触手协调运动
旁白："But when they need to coordinate — open a jar, catch prey — the central brain steps in."
字幕：Central brain = coordinator, not controller

[16-20s] 转折
画面：画面从水下渐变到数字界面
旁白："Now imagine your AI assistant worked this way."
字幕：What if your AI worked like this? 🐙

[20-28s] 映射
画面：八爪鱼图变成 OpenOctopus 架构图，触手 → 域
旁白："Each tentacle is a Realm of your life. Pet. Finance. Work. Legal. Each one thinks independently — its own knowledge, its own agents, its own memory."
字幕：Each tentacle = 1 Realm
  🐾 Pet | 💰 Finance | 💼 Work | ⚖️ Legal

[28-35s] 中枢
画面：中枢大脑发光，协调所有触手
旁白："The central brain only steps in when realms need to talk to each other."
字幕：Cross-realm coordination — only when needed

[35-42s] Summon
画面：一条触手伸向一个灰色实体，触碰后实体亮起变成 Agent
旁白："And the killer feature: Summon. Touch any real-world entity with a tentacle — and it comes alive as an AI agent."
字幕：SUMMON — entities become living agents ✨

[42-48s] CTA
画面：OpenOctopus logo + 真实章鱼叠加
旁白："Inspired by nature. Built for your life. OpenOctopus."
字幕：🐙 OpenOctopus | Open source | Link in bio
```

## 视频制作提示词

### 章鱼触手 → 域映射 变形片段（16-28s）

```
A smooth morphing transition (vertical 9:16). Starting frame: a realistic octopus swimming in dark blue ocean water, tentacles extended. The image gradually transforms — the octopus body becomes a stylized tech illustration, the ocean becomes a dark digital background (#0D1117), each tentacle morphs into a glowing line connecting to a floating holographic card. The cards show domain icons: paw, dollar sign, briefcase, gavel, house, heart, pulse, dumbbell. The octopus head transforms into a central hub with a brain icon. Cyan (#00D4AA) and purple (#6C3FA0) color scheme. Smooth 6-second transition from biology to technology.
```

### 触手触碰实体 → Summon 片段（35-42s）

```
Dark background (9:16 vertical). A glowing purple (#6C3FA0) octopus tentacle extends from the left side of the frame toward a grey, translucent silhouette of a dog in the center. The tentacle tip glows bright cyan (#00D4AA). When it touches the silhouette, a burst of light and particles erupts. The grey silhouette transforms into a colorful, alive golden retriever with a holographic UI ring around it showing personality traits and a chat bubble. The word "SUMMON" appears in large glowing cyan text. Magical, cinematic. 4 seconds.
```

### 生物学科普 — 触手独立神经图（4-12s）

```
An educational illustration (9:16 vertical), dark background. A stylized octopus diagram showing its nervous system. The central brain (mantle) is highlighted with a small glow. Each of the 8 tentacles has its own glowing node representing a "mini-brain" or ganglion. Animated pulses of light travel along the tentacles independently, showing autonomous neural activity. One tentacle is shown separated from the body but still pulsing with its own light. Labels: "Central brain: 150M neurons" pointing to head, "Tentacle ganglia: 350M neurons" pointing to arms. Scientific yet visually appealing. 4 seconds.
```
