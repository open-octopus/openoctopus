# TikTok #03 — Vacation Chaos（趣味 Skit）

> 时长：20-30 秒
> 风格：POV 趣味短剧 + 文字动画
> 目标：高互动率，评论区引爆

---

## 脚本

### 画面 + 旁白

```
[0-3s] 钩子
画面：黑屏 + 大字打出
字幕：POV: You told your AI agents you're going on vacation
音效：紧张的倒计时音效

[3-6s] 狗
画面：一只金毛狗的照片/视频，配上 AI 语音气泡
字幕：🐕 Momo: "WHO WILL FEED ME?! I'm going to STARVE"
音效：狗叫 + 戏剧性音效

[6-9s] 妈妈
画面：一位妈妈的照片/插画，温馨背景
字幕：👩 Mom: "I'll come take care of Momo. And clean your apartment."
音效：温暖提示音

[9-12s] 车
画面：一辆车的仪表盘/外观
字幕：🚗 Car: "Current range: 287km. Charge me before you leave."
音效：电子哔声

[12-15s] 预算
画面：钱包/图表图标
字幕：💰 Budget: "Trip estimate: $800. You have $1,200 left this month."
音效：收银机声

[15-18s] 律师
画面：法律书/天平图标
字幕：⚖️ Lawyer: "Did you buy travel insurance? Check clause 4.2."
音效：严肃敲锤声

[18-22s] 健身教练
画面：哑铃/运动图标
字幕：🏃 Fitness: "There's a gym near your hotel. Don't skip leg day."
音效：哨子声

[22-28s] 系统汇总
画面：所有 Agent 头像从四面八方飞入，汇聚成一份清单
字幕：🐙 System: "Here's your Trip Prep Checklist. Approve?"
旁白/文字："One sentence. Six realms. Zero manual coordination."
音效：完成音效

[28-30s] CTA
画面：OpenOctopus logo
字幕：This is OpenOctopus 🐙 Link in bio
```

### 配乐建议

- 使用 TikTok 热门「chaos」类音效（如 Oh No / Dramatic Sound）
- 或使用快节奏鼓点，每次切换 Agent 对应一个鼓点

---

## 视频制作提示词

### 方案 A：纯文字动画（最易制作）

用 CapCut 制作，无需实拍：

1. 黑色背景 (#0D1117)
2. 每个 Agent 的文字逐条从底部「弹入」
3. 每条配不同的 emoji 头像和边框颜色
4. 最后所有条目飞入中心汇聚成清单
5. 全程配音乐 + 音效

### 方案 B：宠物实拍混剪

1. 拍一段狗狗看着行李箱的真实反应（3-5 秒）
2. 其余 Agent 用文字动画
3. 开头用真实狗狗画面做钩子，完播率更高

### 方案 C：AI 生成片段

#### 多 Agent 混乱响应片段

```
A vertical video (9:16), dark background. Rapid sequence of chat-style message bubbles appearing one after another from different directions — left, right, bottom, top. Each bubble has a different icon (dog, woman, car, dollar, gavel, dumbbell) and a short text message. The bubbles appear with increasing speed, creating a sense of organized chaos. Then all bubbles suddenly fly toward the center and merge into a single glowing checklist document. Cyan (#00D4AA) and purple (#6C3FA0) color scheme. 6 seconds. Energetic, fun, slightly chaotic.
```

#### Agent 头像汇聚片段

```
Dark background (9:16 vertical). Six circular agent avatars scattered around the screen — a dog, a mother figure, a car, a piggy bank, a gavel, a dumbbell. Each avatar has a subtle glow in a different color. They orbit slowly, then one by one fly toward the center, forming a ring around a central octopus logo. When the last avatar joins, the ring pulses with cyan (#00D4AA) light and a checklist icon appears in the center. Magical, satisfying convergence animation. 4 seconds.
```
