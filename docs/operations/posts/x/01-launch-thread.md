## Thread

### 1/6

I'm building OpenOctopus — an open-source AI system that manages your entire life by realms.

Not another chatbox. Not another Notion alternative.

An octopus. Each tentacle governs a life domain. Autonomously.

🧵 Let me explain.

### 2/6

Your life has structure. Pets. Parents. Finance. Work. Legal. Health.

But every AI assistant today throws it all into one chat window.

OpenOctopus organizes life into **Realms** — each with its own knowledge base, agent team, and memory.

Like an octopus: each tentacle has its own nerve center.

[配图：Realm Matrix 信息图]

### 3/6

The killer feature: **Summon**.

Turn any real-world entity into a living AI agent.

Your dog → remembers its vaccine schedule, yells when you forget to feed it
Your mom → reminds you of her birthday preferences, alerts you when it's cold in her city
Your car → tracks maintenance, estimates trip costs

Data becomes alive.

[配图：Summon 概念图]

### 4/6

When you say "I'm traveling next week", all your summoned agents respond:

🐕 Dog: "Who's feeding me for 5 days?!"
👩 Mom: "I can come take care of the dog."
🚗 Car: "Charge to full before departure?"
💰 Budget: "Trip estimate: $X. Save receipts."

→ System compiles a Trip Prep Checklist for your approval.

Cross-realm intelligence. Not siloed bots.

### 5/6

Built with:
- TypeScript + Node.js
- SQLite (local-first) + optional Supabase sync
- Per-realm vector memory (pgvector)
- MCP / A2A protocol support
- RealmHub — install pre-built realm packages in one click

Your data stays on your machine. Always.

### 6/6

OpenOctopus is open source under MIT.

⭐ GitHub: github.com/open-octopus/openoctopus

If you've ever wished your AI assistant understood the *structure* of your life — not just your prompts — come build with us.
